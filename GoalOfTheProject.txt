Goal of the project: 

To create a Track and Field database that is easy to access and easy to read and understand. 

Questions to be answered (College):

- Who is the greatest double in collegiate history?

- What school has the greatest 100m program, 200m program, etc...

- Who is greatest hurdler in collegiate history?

- Which program has the greatest relay program ?

- Who is the greatest collegiate track and field athlete in collegiate history?
